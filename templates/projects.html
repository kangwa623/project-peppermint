Innovation Hub Projects

Innovation Hub UNZA founded in 2023 is a local STEM Hub at the University Of Zambia School Of Engineering. It is a Hub dedicated at promoting student ideas in using local resources to solve local problems. We provide a platform for students to discuss and showcase their creativity and innovative ideas in solving community problems. We do this by conducting workshops, STEM competitions as well as the design and prototyping of technological projects that are aimed at fostering creativity amoung student.

The University of Zambia won first prize at the 2024 Agricultural And Commercial Show in the category of best innovation exhibit.

Innovation Hub with support from The University of Zambia School of Engineering Management has been working on several projects that seek to address the challenges our communities are going through.

Amoung these challenges is a smart egg incubator that uses IoT to monitor temperature and humidity, includes a GSM interface for SMS updates, and automatically turns the eggs.
